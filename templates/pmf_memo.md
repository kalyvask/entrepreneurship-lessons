# PMF Memo — Template

The capstone artifact. A 1–2 page synthesis of the whole journey — insight → who → validation → metrics → decision — that you can hand to an investor, a co-founder candidate, or an early hire to answer one question: **where are you on product-market fit, and what's the evidence?**

This is not a pitch deck. It's an honest internal-grade memo that happens to be shareable. Every claim is backed by evidence or marked as an open hypothesis. The discipline of writing it forces you to confront what you actually know.

Build it from your stage work and grade it against [`rubrics/journey_rubrics.md`](../rubrics/journey_rubrics.md) before you share it.

## How to use it

- **Write it from evidence.** Every claim either cites a behavior/number or is explicitly flagged as an open hypothesis. No happy ears.
- **One page if pre-MVP, two if you have metrics.** If you can't fit it, you're not yet clear enough.
- **Grade before sharing.** Run the relevant stage rubric ([`rubrics/journey_rubrics.md`](../rubrics/journey_rubrics.md)). A memo that claims PMF but scores 2/6 on the Stage 06 rubric will not survive a sharp reader.
- **The surprise section is not optional.** "Savor the surprise" — if you have no surprise to report, you probably haven't looked hard at your data.
- **Update it each stage.** It's a living document; the verdict moves as you learn.
