## Domain note

Your professional background does **not** have to define your direction. Many of the strongest outcomes come from people exploring industries they had no prior experience in — the combination of existing expertise *plus* genuine curiosity about a new space often produces the most original insights. Don't let "but I've only ever worked in X" narrow your haystack prematurely.
