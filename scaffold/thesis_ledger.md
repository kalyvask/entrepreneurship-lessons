# Thesis & Learnings Ledger

This is the **operator-level** layer — distinct from `founder-state.yaml`, which tracks *one
venture*. This file is **yours, across ventures**: the durable lessons you accumulate about what
desperation actually looks like, what bets you back and which you pass, and how you write a value
hypothesis that holds. It is the thing a repeat founder or an investor builds over a career.

Keep one of these per *person*, not per venture. If you run several ventures, this ledger sits
above all of them (keep it in one place you control). The per-venture workspaces feed it.

You don't hand-fill this either. Run `/ent-thesis`: after a pivot, a PMF read, a kill, or a
value-hypothesis exercise, it captures what you learned, appends it with the evidence, and rewrites
the synthesized sections below. The log is append-only; the synthesis is rewritten as it grows.

## Learnings log (append-only)

One row per durable lesson. A lesson earns a row only when a real moment taught it — a pivot, a
failed test, a kept or churned customer, a PMF read. Cite the evidence (which venture, which
artifact). Don't edit past rows; the history is the point.

| Date | Context (venture / stage) | The lesson | Evidence | Tag |
|---|---|---|---|---|
|  |  |  |  | desperation / who / pricing / insight / pivot / style / value-hypothesis |

### Tags

- `desperation` — what real desperation looked like vs. what fooled me
- `who` — narrowing, the over-performing slice, segment boundaries
- `pricing` — willingness-to-pay, packaging, what people actually paid for
- `insight` — inflections that were real vs. consensus dressed up
- `pivot` — change decisions and how they played out
- `style` — my own judgment, edge, and recurring mistakes (feeds the investment memo)
- `value-hypothesis` — what makes a what/who/how + leap of faith hold or break

---

When a lesson and a book disagree, your **logged, evidenced** lesson wins for *you* — that is the
whole point of keeping this. But hold the bar high: a single anecdote is not a lesson. Three
independent moments that point the same way is. And the PMF fundamentals (desperation over need,
iterate the who not the what, narrow, learning rate over shipping rate) are the prior you only
overturn with strong, repeated evidence of your own.
