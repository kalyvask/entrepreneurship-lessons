## Marks 2x2 quadrant check

| | Consensus is right | Consensus is wrong |
|---|---|---|
| **I agree with consensus** | No edge | Wrong with crowd |
| **I disagree with consensus** | Wrong alone (costly) | **Right alone (winning)** |

**Which quadrant am I in?**

- [ ] Right alone (winning) — the place returns live
- [ ] Wrong alone — costly; either change view or accept loss
- [ ] No edge — consensus belief; not worth pursuing
- [ ] Wrong with crowd — uninvested

If you're not in "right alone," you don't have an insight; you have a consensus opinion.

## Excitement check (don't skip this)

If this insight plays out and the resulting business succeeds, what does the world look like in 10 years?

> _______________________________________________________________
> _______________________________________________________________

Am I excited about that future?

- [ ] Excited and motivated
- [ ] Intellectually interested but not personally motivated
- [ ] Mostly attracted to the financial outcome
- [ ] Meh

**Discipline:** if your insight is right and the business succeeds, you'll spend 10 years on it. Excitement matters more than people admit. If "meh" — find a different insight.
